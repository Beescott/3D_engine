=== Movement ===
Forward - W
Backward - S
Left - A
Right - D
To rotate the camera : right click and move the mouse

=== Object===
Select an object - Left click (Click on the sky to deselect)
Scale object - (+ or -)

Move object - (X and/or Y and/or Z) PLUS (+ or -) 
Rotate object - (R) PLUS (X and/or Y and/or Z) PLUS (+ or -)

=== Misc ===
Change perspective (ortho or not) - P
Display / Hide complex object (the book) - M 		- A LOT MORE OF FPS
